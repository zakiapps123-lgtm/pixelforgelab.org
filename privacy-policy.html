Introduction

At PixelForge Lab, we believe in a simple and private gaming experience. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your data security while using our mobile applications.

We have built our games with privacy as a core principle — not an afterthought. Your trust matters to us, and we want to be fully transparent about how (or rather, how little) we interact with your data.

01 🚫

Zero Data Collection

✓ No Data Collected

We respect your privacy. Our games do not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal information from your device.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or locations.
  • We do not use tracking identifiers or analytics tools.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Your gameplay data stays entirely on your device.
02 📵

No Advertising

✓ 100% Ad-Free

To provide a clean and uninterrupted user experience, our games are 100% Ad-Free.

  • We do not display third-party advertisements.
  • We do not share any information with advertising networks because we do not use them.
  • No banner ads, interstitials, or rewarded video ads exist in our games.
03 👶

Children's Privacy

✓ COPPA Compliant

Our games are designed for all ages and are safe for children. Since we do not collect any data, our applications are fully compliant with privacy laws such as COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Parents and guardians can be confident that their children can enjoy our games without any privacy concerns.

04 🔗

Third-Party Links

✓ Self-Contained

Our games are self-contained and do not contain links to external websites or third-party services.

You can play with complete peace of mind knowing that no external parties are involved in your gaming experience.

05 🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices. Continued use of our games after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
